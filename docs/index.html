Our Mission

Why Safe Rec Ventura?

Ventura has thousands of recorded bike and pedestrian crashes, many of which are preventable. We believe every resident deserves to walk, roll, and ride safely. Our goal is to inform the community, collect community voices, and advocate for evidence-based infrastructure improvements.

🗺 Data-Driven Advocacy

We map real crash records from CHP to show exactly where and how crashes happen. By finding the high crash areas, we can turn our data into a call for action.

📣 Community Voice

Your experience matters. We collect stories and observations from residents to complement official crash data and highlight gaps.

🏛 Policy Change

Our website makes it simple for residents to connect directly with local decision makers by providing an email template to send to local representatives.

🚶 Who We Serve

Cyclists, pedestrians, parents walking kids to school, seniors, and anyone who believes the streets should be safe for everyone — not just drivers.
